Help a Fury Friend During This Pandamic

Have you considered

Adopting a pet?

Adding a fury friend to the family is a life change for you and for them. Imagine coming home and someone is waiting happyly just to see you it is priceless. Specially during this pandamic it's been even harder for them to find a home as the adoption rate has decreased 36% compared to last year because of the temporary restrictions and social distancing.

Fostering a pet?

Caring for a fury friend during this time is heroic! Since the adoption process has been harder, the animal fostering system experienced an increased of 21%. This is a great opportunity to help by giving a temporary home to them until they find their forever home!

You can also help by donating to your local animal shelter

Animal shelters always welcome any kind of help that they can get. You could help by donating:

  • Pet food
  • Pet hygine products
  • Pet Toys
  • Money
  • Your time and skills

Your fury friends thank you in advance!
